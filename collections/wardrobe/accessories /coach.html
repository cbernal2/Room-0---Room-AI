Mom's Memory
My friend gave this bag to me. Just because she has a lot of bags.

"This bag smells like a specific chapter of my life—maybe a mix of old receipts, vanilla lip gloss, and ambition. It was the 'grown-up' bag I carried when I was still figuring out what being a grown-up actually meant. The weight of it on my shoulder felt like a badge of office. Those scuffs on the suede? Those are miles walked in shoes I probably shouldn't have been wearing, heading toward places I was desperate to get to. It’s tired now, but it carried everything I needed back then, including my confidence."
